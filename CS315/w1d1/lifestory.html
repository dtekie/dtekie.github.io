My Life Story

My name is Daniel Tekie. I was born and raised in Asmara, capital of Eritrea. My educational background is BSc in Mathematics and BA in accounting. I have worked in various organizations for 16 years assuming different positions in Eritrea and in the United States.

Some of the jobs that I have done include Management Information Systems (MIS) officer in National Commission for Demobilization and reintegration Program, Deputy head of MIS unit in The Ministry of Labor and Social welfare, Trainer, consultant, and Researcher in Eritrean Center for Organizational Excellence. In the United Stated I have worked as Database Administrator in SpendHQ, Georgia. I have been married for 15 years now and I am living a happy life with my wife. Currently, I have been enrolled in the Masters of Software Development program of Maharishi International University to enhance my skill set, and get trained in the latest software development and IT Industry.
